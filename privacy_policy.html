Cookies are small data files which are sent to your browser or device to store and track information about you when you use the Site. Cookies are also used to improve and maintain the functionality of the Site, and to track information such as the number of users of the Site, their frequency of use, the profile of the users and other similar information relating to the use of the Site. Server logs are files and records of activity created by the device that you use the Site. For example, a web server log may record a history of page requests whereas other logs may record errors, access attempts or other information about your device, such as your IP address or your device's cookie settings. We may collect and retain such server logs for administrative, security and troubleshooting purposes.
